Privacy Policy

At SymptomEase, we prioritize your privacy and data security. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when hospitals and patients use our system.

Data Collection

We collect hospital data such as name, registration details, contacts, and medical records as per hospital policy. Patient data includes name, date of birth, symptoms, allergies, medications, and post-appointment details. Additionally, we gather system data like logins, timestamps, device information, and interactions to enhance security and functionality.

Data Protection

To safeguard information, we implement encryption, role-based access controls, secure authentication, and regular audits. Patients have the right to access and review their records, request corrections or deletions as per regulations, and opt out of non-essential data usage.

Data Sharing

We do not sell user data; information is only shared with authorized hospitals, when required by law, or to ensure user safety. Data is retained as required for medical and legal purposes and securely deleted or anonymized when no longer needed.

By using our system, you acknowledge and accept this Privacy Policy.
